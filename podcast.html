Podcast
what is it?
Coconut Yogurt is a podcast I started with my close friend of 14 years, Brittany Do, during quarantine in May 2020.

We started this podcast after realizing the breadth and depth of topics that we discussed each time we reconnected. We wanted a way to keep each other accountable for consistently being introspective and reflective about our life experiences, especially as we begin to navigate the daunting decade of our 20s.

This podcast is our way of both documenting and sharing our collective adventures navigating the classic coming of age experience of being 2nd generation Asian American female creatives in university/postgrad as we discuss topics such as self-development, introspection, love, career, life purpose, spirituality, and coconut yogurt.
